Mission

Curating local experience.
Connecting communities.

We surface the best pop-up events across New York City — from intimate gallery openings to exclusive culinary experiences — so you always discover something worth your time. No algorithms, no noise. Just the best of what's happening, right now.

Process

01

Scrape

Automated crawlers run every morning at 06:00 EST, pulling from 12+ curated sources — museums, galleries, editorial sites, and community boards.

02

Enrich

Events are deduplicated, categorized by type, and enriched with full descriptions, pricing, and venue details from source pages.

03

Serve

Everything is cached in PostgreSQL and served via Vercel Edge Functions — sub-200ms response times, anywhere in the world.
